## Baggage Allowances — Economy Class

Emirates Economy class passengers are entitled to a checked baggage allowance based on the route and fare type.

- **Weight concept (most routes):** 25 kg for Economy Saver and Economy Special fares; 35 kg for Economy Flex fares.
- **Piece concept (Americas routes):** 2 pieces, each up to 23 kg (50 lb), for all Economy fares.
- **Carry-on baggage:** 1 piece up to 7 kg, plus a laptop bag or handbag.

## Baggage Allowances — Business Class

Business class passengers receive a more generous checked baggage allowance.

- **Weight concept:** 40 kg on most routes.
- **Piece concept (Americas routes):** 2 pieces, each up to 32 kg (70 lb).
- **Carry-on baggage:** 2 pieces totalling up to 12 kg.

## Baggage Allowances — First Class

First class passengers receive the highest baggage allowance.

- **Weight concept:** 50 kg on most routes.
- **Piece concept (Americas routes):** 2 pieces, each up to 32 kg (70 lb).
- **Carry-on baggage:** 2 pieces totalling up to 12 kg.

## Excess Baggage Fees

Passengers who exceed their checked baggage allowance will be charged excess baggage fees at check-in or online.

- Excess baggage fees vary by route and are typically charged per kilogram over the allowance.
- Approximate rate: USD 15–35 per additional kg depending on the route.
- Pre-purchasing excess baggage online is significantly cheaper than paying at the airport. Online pre-purchase rates are approximately 50% lower than airport rates.
- Oversized baggage (any piece exceeding 150 cm total dimensions) incurs an additional handling fee.

## Cancellation Policy — Economy Special Fare

Economy Special fares are the most restrictive.

- **Before departure:** No refund. The fare is non-refundable.
- **No-show:** No refund. The booking is forfeited.
- **Changes:** Date and routing changes may be permitted for a fee; subject to fare difference.

## Cancellation Policy — Economy Saver Fare

Economy Saver fares offer limited flexibility.

- **More than 24 hours before departure:** Cancellation fee applies (approximately USD 75–150 depending on route). Remaining balance refunded.
- **Within 24 hours of departure:** No refund.
- **No-show:** No refund.

## Cancellation Policy — Economy Flex Fare

Economy Flex fares are fully flexible.

- **Any time before departure:** Full refund with no cancellation fee.
- **No-show:** Refund may be available subject to a fee; contact Emirates customer service.
- **Changes:** Free date and routing changes (subject to fare difference).

## Refund Timelines and Process

Refunds are processed to the original payment method.

- Credit card refunds: approximately 7–10 business days from approval.
- Debit card refunds: approximately 10–14 business days.
- Refund requests can be submitted online via the Emirates Manage a Booking portal or by contacting Emirates customer service.
- Refunds for tickets purchased through travel agents must be processed through the original agent.
