## Baggage Allowances — International Flights

PIA international passengers are entitled to the following checked baggage allowances.

- **Economy class:** 23 kg per passenger on most international routes.
- **Business class:** 32 kg per passenger on most international routes.
- Routes to/from North America follow the piece concept: Economy 2 pieces × 23 kg; Business 2 pieces × 32 kg.
- **Carry-on baggage:** 1 piece up to 7 kg for Economy; 1 piece up to 10 kg for Business.

## Baggage Allowances — Domestic Flights

PIA domestic passengers receive a lower checked baggage allowance.

- **Economy class:** 20 kg per passenger.
- **Business class:** 30 kg per passenger.
- **Carry-on baggage:** 1 piece up to 7 kg for all domestic classes.

## Excess Baggage Fees

Passengers exceeding their baggage allowance are charged at check-in.

- International excess baggage: approximately USD 8–20 per additional kg depending on the route.
- Domestic excess baggage: approximately PKR 100–300 per additional kg.
- Pre-purchasing excess baggage online before check-in may be available at a discounted rate.

## Cancellation Fees and Refund Eligibility

PIA cancellation fees depend on when the cancellation is made relative to the scheduled departure.

- **More than 72 hours before departure:** 10% of the base fare is charged as a cancellation fee. The remaining balance is refunded.
- **Between 24 and 72 hours before departure:** 25% of the base fare is charged as a cancellation fee.
- **Within 24 hours of departure:** 50% of the base fare is charged as a cancellation fee.
- **No-show (failure to check in):** The full fare is forfeited. No refund.
- Non-refundable promotional fares: Not eligible for any refund regardless of cancellation timing.
- Taxes and government fees are generally refundable even on non-refundable fares.

## Refund Process

- Refund requests can be submitted at any PIA ticketing office, through the booking agent, or via PIA customer service.
- Refunds to credit/debit cards: approximately 15–20 business days.
- Refunds for tickets purchased from travel agents must be processed through the original agent.

## Check-in Time Requirements — International Flights

PIA strongly recommends arriving at the airport well in advance for international travel.

- **Check-in opens:** 3 hours before scheduled departure.
- **Check-in deadline (counter closes):** 60 minutes before scheduled departure. Passengers arriving after this time may be denied boarding.
- **Recommended arrival at airport:** At least 3 hours before departure.
- Passengers travelling to the United Kingdom, United States, or Canada should allow additional time for security and pre-clearance procedures.

## Check-in Time Requirements — Domestic Flights

Domestic check-in requirements are less stringent but PIA still recommends arriving early.

- **Check-in opens:** 2 hours before scheduled departure.
- **Check-in deadline (counter closes):** 30 minutes before scheduled departure.
- **Recommended arrival at airport:** At least 90 minutes before departure.

## Online Check-in

- Online check-in is available from 24 hours to 2 hours before scheduled departure for most flights.
- Passengers must still drop off checked baggage at the airport within the check-in window.
