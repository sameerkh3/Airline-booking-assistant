## Baggage Allowances — Economy Class by Fare Bundle

Qatar Airways Economy class baggage allowance depends on the fare bundle purchased.

- **Economy Lite:** 1 piece up to 23 kg checked baggage. Carry-on: 1 piece up to 7 kg.
- **Economy Classic:** 1 piece up to 23 kg checked baggage. Carry-on: 1 piece up to 7 kg.
- **Economy Comfort:** 2 pieces up to 23 kg each checked baggage. Carry-on: 1 piece up to 7 kg.
- **Economy Elite (fully flexible):** 2 pieces up to 23 kg each checked baggage. Carry-on: 1 piece up to 7 kg.

## Baggage Allowances — Business Class by Fare Bundle

- **Business Lite:** 2 pieces up to 32 kg each. Carry-on: 2 pieces up to 15 kg total.
- **Business Classic:** 2 pieces up to 32 kg each. Carry-on: 2 pieces up to 15 kg total.
- **Business Comfort:** 2 pieces up to 32 kg each. Carry-on: 2 pieces up to 15 kg total.
- **Business Elite:** 2 pieces up to 32 kg each. Carry-on: 2 pieces up to 15 kg total.

## Baggage Allowances — First Class

- 3 pieces up to 32 kg each checked baggage.
- Carry-on: 2 pieces up to 15 kg total.

## Sports Equipment and Special Baggage

Qatar Airways accepts a range of sports and special equipment subject to the following rules.

- Sports equipment (e.g. golf bags, bicycles, skis, surfboards) can be checked in as part of the standard baggage allowance if within the weight and piece limits, or as additional baggage for a fee.
- Golf bags: accepted as one of the standard pieces if within weight limits. Excess golf bag fee: approximately USD 50–100 depending on route.
- Bicycles: must be packed in a hard-sided case or bike box. Accepted as oversized baggage; fees apply.
- Surfboards and ski equipment: accepted as oversized baggage; advance notification recommended.
- Musical instruments: small instruments may be taken as carry-on (subject to size). Large instruments require a separate seat purchase or checked baggage handling.

## Cancellation Policy — Economy Lite

Economy Lite is Qatar Airways' most restrictive fare.

- **Non-refundable.** No cash refund for cancellations.
- Taxes and government-levied fees may be refundable; contact Qatar Airways for details.
- **Date changes:** Not permitted on most routes. Subject to fare rules.

## Cancellation Policy — Economy Classic

- Cancellation fee applies (approximately USD 100–200 depending on route and time of cancellation).
- Remaining fare value may be refunded or converted to travel credit.

## Cancellation Policy — Economy Comfort

- Cancellations permitted with a lower fee than Classic (approximately USD 50–100).
- Remaining balance refunded to original payment method.

## Cancellation Policy — Economy Elite

- Fully refundable. No cancellation fee.
- Full refund to original payment method regardless of when the cancellation is made before departure.

## No-Show Policy

If a passenger fails to check in or board the flight without cancelling in advance:

- **Economy Lite and Classic:** The booking is forfeited. No refund or rebooking permitted.
- **Economy Comfort and Elite:** A no-show fee applies (approximately USD 150). Remaining balance may be refunded or used for rebooking.
- **Business and First class:** No-show fees apply; contact Qatar Airways for specific amounts.
- Onward connecting flights in the same booking may be cancelled if the first segment is a no-show.

## Refund Timelines

- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7–14 business days of approval.
- Requests submitted online via Manage Booking or through Qatar Airways customer service.
- Travel agent bookings must be refunded through the booking agent.
